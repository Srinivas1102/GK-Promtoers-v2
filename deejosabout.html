About Us

Incorporated in the year 2008, DEEJOS has been in the field of Constrcution & Architecture for 15 Years now. With more than a Decade of extensive experience, DEEJOS has a proven history of accomplishing many complex projects be it Luxury individual bungalows or massive Government Contracts.

Founded by First Generation Entreprenuer Mr.Dennis Ebenezar, DEEEJOS is now emerged as the most successful Architectural firm in India under his effective management.

We always believe in team work and yes more than a team we are a family. We are a friendly, open-minded team of Architects whose work is inspired by people and culture. We take every opportunity to add beauty along the way.

Whatever the project - big or small, bold or sensitive, we answer your equirements withour ever losing sight of your practical, budgetary or commercial needs or wants.
